## The Problems We Solve

### Primary Problems

#### 1. **Contract Portfolio Invisibility**
**Problem:** Organizations manage hundreds to thousands of active contracts (vendors, consultants, construction, IT, professional services) with no unified view of performance, compliance status, or upcoming obligations.

**Impact:**
- Can't answer "How are our vendors performing?" without weeks of manual research
- Renewal dates missed, resulting in unfavorable auto-renewals or service gaps
- Non-compliant vendors discovered only during audits
- No portfolio-level view of risk exposure or spend concentration

#### 2. **Reactive Compliance & Risk Management**
**Problem:** Contract compliance violations, insurance lapses, missed deliverables, and performance issues discovered too late - often during audits, after incidents, or when damage is done.

**Impact:**
- **$50K-500K+ per incident** in audit findings, penalties, legal costs
- **Insurance gaps** exposing organization to liability (discovered post-accident)
- **Regulatory violations** (DBE/MBE goals, wage requirements, safety standards)
- **Vendor performance degradation** unnoticed until service failures
- **Contract disputes** due to poor documentation and tracking

#### 3. **Manual, Time-Consuming Contract Administration**
**Problem:** Contract managers spend 40-60% of their time on manual tasks: tracking deliverables in spreadsheets, chasing vendors for insurance certificates, compiling compliance reports, responding to audit requests.

**Impact:**
- 20-40 hours per month per contract manager on administrative overhead
- Procurement staff burned out, high turnover
- Strategic work (vendor relationship management, negotiation, improvement) neglected
- Scalability ceiling - can't manage more contracts without adding headcount

#### 4. **Fragmented Data & Systems**
**Problem:** Contract data scattered across: procurement system, finance/ERP, SharePoint, email, paper files, individual laptops. No single source of truth. Different departments track different aspects.

**Impact:**
- Duplicate effort across departments
- Inconsistent data, conflicting information
- Audit prep takes weeks of data gathering
- Can't perform portfolio analytics or benchmarking
- Knowledge loss when staff leave

#### 5. **Poor Vendor Performance Management**
**Problem:** No systematic way to track and score vendor performance across contracts. Evaluation based on recent memory, anecdotal complaints, or not done at all. Past performance not considered in future procurements.

**Impact:**
- Repeat awards to underperforming vendors
- High-performing vendors not recognized or rewarded
- Inability to justify vendor decisions with data
- No continuous improvement feedback loop
- Competitive procurement protests due to lack of documented evaluation

#### 6. **Spend & Value Optimization Blindness**
**Problem:** Organizations can't identify consolidation opportunities, volume discount potential, or poor value contracts because they lack portfolio-level visibility and analytics.

**Impact:**
- 15-30% of contract spend could be optimized through consolidation
- Duplicate or redundant contracts for similar services
- Small-dollar contracts proliferate inefficiently
- Missed volume discount opportunities
- No strategic vendor relationship management

## The Solution: Contract Oversight System

### What We Do

- **Predictive analytics** - identify contracts likely to experience problems
- **Spend concentration** - flag over-reliance on single vendors

- **Portfolio dashboards** - executive view of risk exposure

#### 5. **Spend Analytics & Optimization**

- **Duplicate detection** - identify redundant contracts for consolidation

- **Vendor consolidation analysis** - recommend strategic sourcing opportunities

- **Savings tracking** - measure contract negotiation and optimization outcomes
- **Cost avoidance** - quantify value of compliance monitoring (penalties avoided)

#### 6. **Workflow Automation**
- **Contract approval routing** - automated based on value, type, risk
- **Amendment processing** - track changes, update records automatically

- **Document analysis** - AI extracts obligations, risk clauses, non-standard terms
- **Recommendation engine** - suggests actions (renew, rebid, consolidate, terminate)
- **Predictive analytics** - forecast vendor failures, compliance lapses

#### 8. **Audit & Regulatory Compliance**
