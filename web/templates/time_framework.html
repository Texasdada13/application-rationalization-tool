What is the TIME Framework?

The TIME Framework is a strategic tool for application portfolio rationalization that categorizes applications based on their business value and technical health. This quadrant-based approach helps executives make data-driven decisions about where to allocate resources and which applications require immediate attention.

TIME Framework Visualization

Invest Strategy

High Business Value, High Technical Health

  • Increase funding for feature development and innovation
  • Expand capabilities to support strategic business goals
  • Maintain technical excellence through continuous improvement
  • Leverage as platforms for competitive advantage

Tolerate Strategy

Low Business Value, High Technical Health

  • Minimize operational and maintenance costs
  • Continue running but avoid new feature development
  • Monitor for potential consolidation opportunities
  • Consider as candidates for standardization

Migrate Strategy

High Business Value, Low Technical Health

  • Prioritize for modernization or replacement
  • Address technical debt and security vulnerabilities
  • Evaluate cloud migration or platform upgrade options
  • Allocate resources for technical improvement projects

Eliminate Strategy

Low Business Value, Low Technical Health

  • Develop retirement and decommissioning plans
  • Identify alternative solutions or consolidation options
  • Plan data migration and business process transitions
  • Capture cost savings from elimination
